About KEEP-ID

KEEP-ID is a unified digital government identity and registration-tracking platform designed to simplify how citizens interact with government services.

We understand the frustration of long queues, uncertain waiting times, and the stress of not knowing when your ID will be ready. That's why we created a single, trusted platform where you can track all your government IDs in real-time.

Our mission is to provide every citizen with a seamless, secure, and human-centered experience when accessing government services.
